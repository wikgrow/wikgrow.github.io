Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

At Wikgrow, accessible from wikgrow.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Wikgrow and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you fill out our contact or quotation form, such as your name, phone number, email address, and any project details you submit.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our services.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our website experience.
  • Understand and analyze how you use our website.
  • Communicate with you regarding project inquiries or customer support.

3. Log Files and Analytics

Wikgrow follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, and referring/exit pages.

4. Third-Party Links

Our website features social buttons (like WhatsApp) or links to external client platforms. We do not control the privacy policies of third-party platforms, and we encourage you to review them directly.

5. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at our provided support channels.
